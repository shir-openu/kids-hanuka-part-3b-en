Excellent! The proof is correct!
Explanation:
1. AB = CD - because all sides of a square are equal
2. AF = CE - because F is the midpoint of AD and E is the midpoint of BC, so both equal half the side length
3. ∠BAF = ∠DCE = 90° - because all angles in a square are right angles
4. Therefore the triangles are congruent by SAS (Side-Angle-Side)
